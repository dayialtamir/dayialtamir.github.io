Ecuador

I was born in the USA and raised in Ecuador. I was nine when my parents decided to leave USA and start a new life in Ecuador. I still remember when they gave me the "good" news, which at that time I did not think of them as good at all, however, little did I know that soon I was going to fall in love with this beautiful country and never want to come back.

In Ecuador, your friends are your family. When you meet someone for the first time, know that you have now become "un amigo" for them. Don't be surprised that those friends, may later show up at your house just to say hi or to join you at family meals. This is why mothers are always prepared and have extra food for last-minute visits.

A wonderful thing that this country has is its delicious food, and that everything revolves around it. You have at least three meals a day, and these are not simple meals, but elaborated meals. However, those are not the only meals you might have. A friend might stop by to say hi and he will probably invite you for some "snack" at some kiosk also know as "a hole in the wall". If you ever decide to go visit Ecuador, don't forget to have a dish named "Arroz con menestra y carne asada"; you will love it!

A funny thing about living in Ecuador is that if you need a favor you will always find someone to do it for you, whether it is your family or friends, or the friends of your friends, which in the end will also become your friends.

As you can see in Ecuador you will never feel lonely because even if for some strange reason your family forgets about you, I assure you that your neighbors won't, which by the way I forgot to mention, "your neighbors are also considered friends".

I truly miss Ecuador and I wish soon I will be able to visit my friends and family.
